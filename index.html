About Us

What started out as a simple idea for the Hintonburg/Westboro community from owner Simon Saab, turned out to be a blessing for the whole Ottawa region. The first day The Table Restaurant opened it's doors back in 2000, was an unexpected one for all. People responded quickly and in large numbers, and a choice vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free destination was born.

Simon Saab

Owner and Proprietor Simon Saab, has been in the hospitality industry for over 35 years, with an early start in his father's restaurant in Sandy Hill.

Ian Lipski

Head Chef Ian Lipski was trained in London England in French Haute Cuisine, and has since worked in Naussau, Bahamas and Canada.
